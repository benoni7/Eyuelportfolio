About Me

From my early years at Vision Academy High School in Addis Ababa, I’ve always been driven by the desire to create change. As a teenager with a vision for the future, I’ve focused on learning the skills necessary to shape the digital world. My passion for technology, design, and marketing has fueled my journey toward building a career where I can help businesses grow and evolve in an increasingly digital space.

During my time at Vision Academy, I started to develop a keen interest in digital technologies. This led me to pursue certifications and hands-on projects that allowed me to transform my passion into practical skills. I earned certifications in Programming Fundamentals from Udacity, where I gained the foundation to build interactive and responsive websites. I also pursued Figma Design at Alison, learning how to craft intuitive and visually engaging user interfaces. Additionally, I completed a Digital Marketing certification from UniAthena, equipping myself with the knowledge to help brands optimize their online presence and drive customer engagement.

I’ve had the opportunity to apply these skills in real-world projects, such as the BENONI Gym and Fitness Website and an E-Commerce Website, where I focused on both the technical and design aspects, ensuring a seamless user experience. These projects reflect my belief that technology can bring about positive change and create impactful experiences.

As a teenager striving for change, I am dedicated to continuous learning and growth. I aim to push the boundaries of what’s possible with every project, and I’m excited to bring innovative ideas to life in the ever-evolving digital landscape.
